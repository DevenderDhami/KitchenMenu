Rules and Regulations

  1. Shared Space: The kitchen is a shared space, and all roommates have equal access and rights to use it.
  2. Cleaning Responsibilities: Each roommate is responsible for cleaning up after themselves immediately after using the kitchen. Dishes should be washed and put away, and countertops should be wiped clean.
  3. Cooking and Food Storage: Ensure that all food items are properly sealed and stored to avoid pests.
  4. Sharing and Communication: When preparing a meal, if you expect to use a significant amount of kitchen space, please inform your roommates. Coordinate and communicate to avoid conflicts.
  5. Food Disposal: Dispose of trash and food waste properly. Use designated trash containers and recycling bins. Keep the kitchen free from pests by not leaving food scraps or trash out.
  6. Kitchen Supplies: Shared kitchen supplies such as pots, pans, utensils, and cleaning materials should be used with care and promptly cleaned and returned to their designated locations.
  7. Respect for Others: Be mindful of other roommates when using the kitchen, especially during early morning or late-night hours.
  8. Conflict Resolution: If any issues or disputes arise concerning the kitchen, roommates should address them through open communication and compromise.
  9. Sanctions: Violation of these kitchen rules may result in sanctions, including warnings, chore assignments, or financial penalties, depending on the severity of the violation.
